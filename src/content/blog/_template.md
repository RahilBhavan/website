## Introduction

**Hook:** Start here with a compelling opening that grabs attention. This could be a question, a personal story, a surprising statistic, or a bold statement.

**Context:** Explain why this topic matters. What problem does it solve? Why should readers care? What's the current state of things?

**Preview:** Give readers a roadmap. What will they learn? What questions will be answered? This sets expectations and helps readers decide if they want to continue.
