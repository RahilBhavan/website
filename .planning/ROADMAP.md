# Roadmap: Professional Blog & Portfolio

## Overview

Build a professional blog and portfolio platform from foundation to launch, optimized for effortless writing and publishing. Starting with technology choices and design system, progressing through blog and project showcase features, then optimizing for performance, SEO, and analytics before polishing the content creation workflow.

None
